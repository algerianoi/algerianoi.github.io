Competitions

In order to represent Algeria in High School programming contests, you need to join our training program on our Discord server.

Algeria is currently taking part in 5 main competitions :

  • IOI (International Olympiad in Informatics) : A yearly competition, generally between August and September, in which around 90 countries take part, each with a team of 4 students who compete individually aiming for medals.
  • AFPC (Arab Future Programmers Competition) : This competition is held in April of every year in Amman, Jordan in which around 10 Arab countries take part with up to 3 teams of 3 students who work together during the contest, in addition to many Jordanian teams.
  • EGOI (European Girls Olympiad in Informatics) : A competition exclusively for girls in which each country sends a team of 4 contestants who compete individually.
  • CEOI (Centro-European Olympiad in Informatics) : A competition with a higher difficulty than IOI in which countries of central Europe, and some guest countries like Algeria, reale part with a team of 4 students who compete individually.
  • APIO (Asia-Pacific Informatics Olympiad) : A 1-day online competition with up to 60 contestants per country who compete individually.
